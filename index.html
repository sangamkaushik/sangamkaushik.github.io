My Story

I am a web developer with experience in Ruby and JavaScript. Graduating in Mechanical Engineering with a specialization in Mechatronics, the major focus of my degree has been robotics and automation. Somewhere along that course, I fell in love with coding. I was introduced to technical trading (day trading/stock trading) during 2nd year of university by a friend. Having had some experience in programming, I began to develop technical indicators and trading strategies in C#.

After graduation I moved to Calgary, Alberta in pursuit of Mechanical Engineering roles in the lucrative Canadian Oil Sands Industry. During an interview, I was told that the company needed to optimize their logistical network as they were overspending by an estimated $22 million. I knew how to solve the problem, in theory that is, and wanted to be able to automate the decision process. I felt helpless, as I only knew how to write scripts and did not know how to write large-scale software. At this point, more than anything else, I wanted to learn to code!

I then applied to bootcamps in San Francisco to expedite my learning process and got into App Academy (my top choice). I am now looking forward to develop meaningful software that optimizes solutions.
